Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

CamCoach ("the app") is developed by Ola Opheim.

Data collection

CamCoach does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. No analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising SDKs.

Camera access

The app uses your camera to analyse the live viewfinder and display a real-time score and tip. Camera frames are processed entirely on-device using Apple's Vision and AVFoundation frameworks. No images are saved or sent anywhere without your explicit action.

Photo library access

If you choose to review a photo from your library, that photo is analysed on-device. It is not copied, uploaded, or retained by the app after the session ends.

On-device processing

All scoring and analysis runs locally on your device using native Apple frameworks. The app makes no network requests.

Third-party services

The app does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.
